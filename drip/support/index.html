Common questions

Is this app reviewed by a clinician?

No. The app implements standard nursing-school formulas (ISMP guidelines, Calculate with Confidence by Deborah Gray Morris, and equivalents) and is intended as an educational reference tool. It is not a clinical decision support system. Always verify against your institution's protocols.

My tubing has a different drop factor than the ones shown.

Drop factors vary by manufacturer. The app supports the four most common values (10, 15, 20, 60 gtt/mL). If your tubing uses a different factor, custom drop factor support is on the roadmap. Email us and we'll prioritize it.

Will there be an Android version?

Planned. v1.x is iOS-only while we validate the design with real users.
